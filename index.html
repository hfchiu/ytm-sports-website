關於我們 About Us

油尖區康樂體育會，會址座落於油麻地咸美頓街38號，於1995年落成，樓高三層，主要設施包括有康樂活動室兩個及標準網球場兩個。本會於1981年蒙民政事務總署油麻地民政事務處協助成立，為非牟利團體。其目的是推廣油尖區內康樂體育及文娛活動，成立至今已有三十五年。

本會是油尖旺區議會屬下特定團體，每年接受區議會撥款資助以主辦康體活動，例如兒童網球班、健康18式、太極班及網球比賽等等。務求以最低廉的收費，讓一般基層市民有機會參與，以達到推廣及促進社區層面的體育發展。

其中猶以兒童網球班更獲好評。由基本訓練開始，提昇小朋友對網球活動的興趣，有僭質者有機會被挑選作進一步培訓。此外，亦與康樂及文化事務署合辦多項區內體育活動例如有：羽毛球比賽、乒乓球比賽、網球、籃球比賽及游泳比賽等等，參與人數以千計。

本會擁有區內少有之室內網球場，是香港網球總會會員，每年都派隊參加網球總會舉辦的聯賽，本會球場亦是網總聯賽比賽場地之一。本會希望能善用場地以主辦更多大型比賽及多元化活動。

Yaumate & Tsimshatsui Recreation & Sports Association is located at 38 Hamilton Street, Yaumatei. The building was completed in 1995, standing three stories high, with main facilities including two recreation rooms and two standard tennis courts. Our association was established in 1981 with assistance from the Yaumatei Home Affairs Office under the Home Affairs Department, as a non-profit organization. Our purpose is to promote recreational sports and entertainment activities in the Yau Tsim district. We have been established for thirty-five years.

We are a designated organization under the Yau Tsim Mong District Council, receiving annual funding from the District Council to organize recreational and sports activities, such as children's tennis classes, Health 18 exercises, Tai Chi classes, and tennis competitions. We aim to provide the lowest possible fees to enable grassroots citizens to participate, thereby promoting and advancing community-level sports development.

Among these, our children's tennis classes are particularly well-received. Starting from basic training, we enhance children's interest in tennis activities, and those with talent have the opportunity to be selected for further training. Additionally, we co-organize various district sports activities with the Leisure and Cultural Services Department, including badminton competitions, table tennis competitions, tennis, basketball competitions, and swimming competitions, with thousands of participants.

We possess one of the few indoor tennis courts in the district and are members of the Hong Kong Tennis Association. We send teams annually to participate in leagues organized by the Tennis Association, and our courts serve as one of the venues for Tennis Association league matches. We hope to make good use of our facilities to organize more large-scale competitions and diversified activities.
