關於我們 About Us

油尖區康樂體育會有限公司是香港一個致力於推廣體育運動的組織。我們的使命是透過體育活動促進社區健康，培養青少年的體育精神和團隊合作能力。

本會積極參與各項體育活動，包括與東分區委員會合辦運動體驗日、舉辦全港中小學地壺比賽、回歸盃全港地壺比賽、全港18歐組比賽，以及參加18區龍舟比賽等。

我們相信體育運動不僅能強身健體，更能培養品格，建立友誼，促進社區和諧。歡迎各界人士加入我們的大家庭！

Yaumate & Tsimshatsui Recreation & Sports Association Ltd. is an organization dedicated to promoting sports in Hong Kong. Our mission is to foster community health through sports activities and cultivate sportsmanship and teamwork among young people.

We actively participate in various sports activities, including co-organizing sports experience days with the Eastern District Committee, hosting Hong Kong primary and secondary school curling competitions, Reunification Cup Hong Kong curling competition, Hong Kong 18 European group competition, and participating in the 18 districts dragon boat race.

We believe that sports not only strengthen the body but also build character, forge friendships, and promote community harmony. We welcome people from all walks of life to join our big family!

大型活動 Major Events

18區龍舟比賽

18 Districts Dragon Boat Race

2024年6月8日 | June 8, 2024

本會將聯同旺角文娛康樂體育會及九龍總商會組織龍舟隊參加18區龍舟比賽。

Our association will join with Mong Kok Recreation & Sports Association and Kowloon Chamber of Commerce to organize a dragon boat team for the 18 districts dragon boat race.

全運啟航油尖旺東分區運動體驗日

Yau Tsim Mong East District Sports Experience Day

2024年7月6日 | July 6, 2024

與東分區委員會合辦運動體驗日，活動項目包括：3人籃球、欖球、劍擊及手球。本會作為合辦機構將負責聯絡各體育總會合作事宜。

Co-organized with the Eastern District Committee, featuring 3-on-3 basketball, rugby, fencing, and handball. Our association will coordinate with various sports federations as a co-organizer.

回歸盃全港地壺比賽

Reunification Cup Hong Kong Curling Competition

2024年7月26日 | July 26, 2024

全港性地壺比賽，慶祝香港回歸，推廣地壺運動。

Hong Kong-wide curling competition celebrating Hong Kong's reunification and promoting curling sports.

全港18歐組比賽

Hong Kong 18 European Group Competition

2024年8月9日 | August 9, 2024

全港18歐組體育比賽，促進不同年齡組別的體育交流。

Hong Kong 18 European group sports competition, promoting sports exchange among different age groups.

全港中小學地壺比賽

Hong Kong Primary & Secondary School Curling Competition

即將公佈 | To be announced

舉辦全港中小學地壺比賽，並優化及增加國安法教育內容，推廣青少年體育發展。

Organizing Hong Kong primary and secondary school curling competition with enhanced National Security Law education content to promote youth sports development.
